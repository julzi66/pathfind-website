About PathFind

A centralized platform for youth opportunities.

PathFind helps private institutions, NGOs, scholarship providers, and organizations share verified opportunities, while helping students discover scholarships, internships, leadership programs, civic engagement initiatives, and volunteer work.

Since these announcements are often scattered across different pages, websites, and social media posts, PathFind gives institutions one place to post and verify opportunities while helping students avoid missed deadlines.

Mission

To help private institutions publish verified opportunities and connect students with the right paths for education, leadership, service, and community growth.

Vision

A future where institutions can reach the right students and every young person can easily discover opportunities that support their potential, career growth, leadership, and community participation.
